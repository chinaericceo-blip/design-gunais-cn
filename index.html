OUR STORY

GUNAIS Design (谷奈斯空间设计) was founded by designer Eric Y (邵阳), a veteran of over 15 years in architectural and interior design. The firm specializes in high-quality commercial space design, construction drawing deepening, and EPC design management.


With a philosophy rooted in rationality, restraint, and experiential depth, GUNAIS creates spaces that fuse commercial value with humanistic care — where every project tells a story of precision and artistry.


From landmark commercial complexes and office buildings to hotel interiors and exhibition spaces, GUNAIS has earned a reputation for "refined design that creates high-quality spaces."

CORE SERVICES
SERVICES
COMMERCIAL SPACE DESIGN

GUNAIS collaborates with leading clients across commercial complexes, retail chains, and brand flagships to deliver immersive commercial environments that balance aesthetic ambition with operational efficiency.

INTERIOR DESIGN

From concept to completion, GUNAIS delivers full-scope interior design for luxury hotels, resorts, corporate offices, cultural institutions, and premium residences — at any scale.

CONSTRUCTION DRAWING DEEPENING

GUNAIS provides precision construction documentation services, translating conceptual designs into detailed, buildable drawings that ensure quality execution from ground-up.

EPC DESIGN MANAGEMENT

Our EPC management model integrates engineering, procurement, and construction coordination under one design-led framework — minimizing errors, reducing waste, and delivering projects on time.

HOSPITALITY DESIGN

Drawing from extensive experience in international hotel and resort projects, GUNAIS creates hospitality environments that elevate guest experience through thoughtful spatial storytelling.

EXHIBITION & SHOWROOM DESIGN

From brand pavilions to permanent cultural exhibitions, GUNAIS designs immersive showroom environments that communicate brand values through space, light, and material.

HERITAGE RESTORATION

GUNAIS has participated in the restoration of heritage structures and ancient architecture, applying rigorous research and technical mastery to preserve cultural legacy with modern craftsmanship.

ON-SITE TECHNICAL SERVICES

After project commencement, GUNAIS dispatches project designers to site regularly, reporting back to clients on construction progress and quality — ensuring alignment with design intent until final handover.
